## Overview
A "Digital Sanctuary" aesthetic. Focus on high legibility, soft shadows, and a professional blue-accented palette.

## Colors
- **Primary**: The main driver for "Borrow" and "Login" actions.
- **Background**: Off-white to reduce eye strain.
- **Surface**: Used for cards with subtle borders.

## Components
- **Cards**: Soft corners (`rounded.md`) and light shadows.
- **Tables**: Clean lines, no unnecessary borders, highlighted headers.
- **Buttons**: Clear hierarchy between primary and secondary actions.
